How to signup in Waitabit

Here is a quick step-by-step guide on how to sign up in Waitabit for the first time.

  1. Launch Waitabit
  2. Click on 'Signup for Free' from the top right corner or on the hero section. You'll be presented with a pop up for registration. Click on 'Get Started'
  3. A new 12-word phrase gets generated for you.

    4. Note: this is your private key. Copy the secret key and save it.
